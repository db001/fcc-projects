Nikola Tesla

Inventor, Engineer, Futurist, Mad Scientist

Nikola Tesla (10 July 1856 – 7 January 1943) was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, physicist, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

List of Inventions and Achievements

  • 1856 - Tesla Born to Serbian parents in what is now modern-day Croatia
  • 1875 - Tesla begins technical schooling in Graz, Austria
  • 1880 - Tesla begins working for the American Telephone Company in Budapest
  • 1882 - Tesla conceives of AC induction motor 
  • 1882 - Tesla begins working for Edison (in Paris, France)
  • 1883 - Tesla constructs the first polyphase AC motor
  • 1884 - Tesla immigrates to the United States
  • 1884 - Tesla begins working for Thomas Edison
  • 1885 - Tesla quits working for Edison
  • 1886 - Tesla launches Tesla Electric Light & Manufacturing
  • 1887 - Tesla builds first AC Induction Motor
  • 1888 - Tesla demonstrates AC motors to the American Institute of Electrical Engineers (AIEE)
  • 1888 - Inventor and businessman George Westinghouse buys Tesla's key Polyphase Patents after buying patent for the AC transformer
  • 1888 - Tesla begins working for Westinghouse
  • 1891 - Tesla becomes a US citizen 
  • 1891 - Tesla invents 2-coil “Tesla Coil” 
  • 1892 - First Polyphase Power System Installed
  • 1892 - Tesla discovers X-ray radiation while experimenting with HV and evacuated tubes
  • 1893 - Demonstrates Wireless Transmitter/Receiver
  • 1893 - Lighting the 1893 World's Fair Exposition
  • 1894 - Tesla generates 1,000,000 volts
  • 1895 - Tesla harnesses Niagara Falls
  • 1895 - Fire destroys Tesla's laboratory
  • 1896 - First long-distance transmission of polyphase AC Power
  • 1897 - Tesla files the basic Radio Patent
  • 1897 - Tesla releases Westinghouse from contract 
  • 1898 - Tesla Demonstrates Wireless Control 
  • 1898 - Tesla develops electric Ignitor for Gasoline Engines
  • 1899 - Tesla begins his Colorado Springs Research
  • 1900 - Tesla begins Wardenclyffe Transmitter
  • 1903 - Tesla runs out of money 
  • 1904 - Marconi awarded patent for radio
  • 1905 - Wardenclyffe forced to close operations
  • 1906 - Tesla announces Bladeless Turbine 
  • 1907 - During the financial panic of 1907, Tesla agrees to release Westinghouse from paying royalties on every induction motor
  • 1909 - Marconi wins Nobel Prize for Radio
  • 1910-11 - Tesla's bladeless turbines are tested in New York
  • 1915 - Tesla signs over Wardenclyffe deed to pay his debts
  • 1915 - Tesla files lawsuit against Marconi
  • 1916 - Tesla declares bankruptcy
  • 1917 - Wardenclyffe Tower is demolished 
  • 1917 - Tesla receives the Edison Medal 
  • 1928 - Tesla receives his last patent at age 72
  • 1931 - Tesla turns 75
  • 1935 - 15 out of 16 of Marconi's Patent claims are invalidated by the Court of Claims
  • 1943 - Tesla dies penniless in NY hotel on January 7, 1943 
  • 1943 - Tesla awarded Patent for Radio, June 21, 1943
  • 1956 - The "Tesla", a new unit of magnetic flux density in the metric system, is named in Nikola Tesla's honor
